### <Icon name='list' /> The Main Accounts List

This is your central hub for viewing all your financial accounts at a glance. Each account is displayed on a card showing its current **Balance**, total **Income**, and total **Expense** for the current period, giving you a quick check on its health and activity.

<FeatureLink href='/accounts' title='Go to the Accounts Page' icon='landmark'>
  From here, you can search for accounts, create new ones, or add a transaction to any account.
</FeatureLink>

<Step number={1} title='Create a New Account'>
  Click the **"Create Account"** button. You'll need to provide a name, its current starting
  balance, and the currency.
</Step>

<Step number={2} title='Perform Quick Actions'>
  Hover over any account card to reveal quick action buttons. You can **Edit**{' '}
  <Icon name='edit' className='inline h-4 w-4' /> the account's name or **Delete**{' '}
  <Icon name='trash' className='inline h-4 w-4' /> it directly from this view.
</Step>

<Alert variant='destructive' title='Warning: Deleting an Account is Permanent'>
  Deleting an account will also permanently erase **all of its associated transactions**. This
  action cannot be undone. Please be absolutely certain before you confirm.
</Alert>
