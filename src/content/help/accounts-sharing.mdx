### <Icon name='share2' /> Account Sharing

Securely share specific accounts with a partner, accountant, or family member without giving them access to your entire profile.

<FeatureLink href='/shared-accounts' title='Manage Your Shared Accounts' icon='users2'>
  Control who has access to your accounts and view accounts that have been shared with you.
</FeatureLink>

<Step number={1} title='Sharing an Account'>
  From the **Account Details** page, click the **Share**{' '}
  <Icon name='share' className='inline h-4 w-4' /> icon. You will be prompted to enter the email
  address of the user you wish to invite. They will receive an email notification.
</Step>

<Step number={2} title='Managing Access'>
  Navigate to the **Account Shares** page by clicking the **View Shares**{' '}
  <Icon name='groups' className='inline h-4 w-4' /> button on the Account Details page. Here, you
  can see a list of everyone who has access. As the owner, you can **Revoke** their access at any
  time.
</Step>

<Step number={3} title="Viewing 'Shared With Me' Accounts">
  The **Shared Accounts** page from the main sidebar lists all accounts other users have shared with
  you. This keeps them separate from your personal accounts for privacy and clarity.
</Step>
