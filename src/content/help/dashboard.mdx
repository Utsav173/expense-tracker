## <Icon name="layoutGrid" /> The Dashboard Page

The Dashboard is your mission control. It consolidates data from all your accounts to give you a comprehensive, real-time overview of your entire financial situation.

<a id="dashboard-financial-snapshot"></a>
### Financial Snapshot & Quick Stats
This area provides your most critical financial vital signs at a glance.
*   **Overall Net Balance:** The total sum of all your account balances. This is a great indicator of your liquid net worth.
*   **Total Income/Expense:** Shows your total cash inflow and outflow for the time period selected in the Trends chart.
*   **Highest/Lowest Transactions:** Pinpoints your single largest and smallest income and expense transactions.

<a id="dashboard-financial-trends"></a>
### Financial Trends Chart
This powerful chart visualizes the rhythm of your finances over time.
*   **Data Plotted:** It shows your total **Income**, total **Expenses**, and your overall **Balance** across all accounts.
*   **Interactive Views:** You can switch between **Line**, **Bar**, and **Area** chart views to find the visualization that tells your story best.

<a id="dashboard-spending-breakdown"></a>
### Spending Breakdown
This chart is crucial for answering the question: "Where is my money *really* going?"
*   **Function:** It aggregates all your expenses, groups them by category, and displays the proportion of your spending for each.
*   **Chart Types:** Switch between **Pie**, **Donut**, and **Column** views.
