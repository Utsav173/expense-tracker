### <Icon name="search" /> The Account Details Page

Clicking on any account card from the list takes you to its dedicated detail page for an in-depth analysis. All charts, stats, and the transaction list on this page are filtered to show data *only* for the selected account.

<Step number={1} title="Analyze Focused Analytics">
  The **Financial Snapshot** and **Trends Charts** at the top of the page are specific to this account. This is perfect for deep-diving into the performance and history of a single account without the noise of others.
</Step>

<Step number={2} title="Use the Header Actions">
  At the top of the page, you'll find a set of powerful tools specific to this account:
  - <Icon name="download2" className="inline h-4 w-4" /> **Export:** Instantly download a PDF of the current view.
  - <Icon name="statement" className="inline h-4 w-4" /> **Statement:** Go to the dedicated page to generate a formal PDF or XLSX statement.
  - <Icon name="groups" className="inline h-4 w-4" /> **View Shares:** See and manage who you have shared this account with.
  - <Icon name="share" className="inline h-4 w-4" /> **Share:** Invite another user to view this account's data.
</Step>
