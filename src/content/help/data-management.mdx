## <Icon name="database" /> Data Import & Export

Expense Pro gives you full control over your financial data.

<a id="data-management-import"></a>
### Import Transactions Page (`/transactions/import`)
This is the fastest way to get your historical data into the app.
1.  **Step 1: Upload:** Select the Destination Account, then provide your file.
    *   **Excel (XLSX):** For best results, **download our template** and fill it out.
    *   **PDF:** Upload a standard bank statement, and our AI will extract the transactions.
2.  **Step 2: Review:** A table will show the extracted transactions. Review them for accuracy and uncheck any you wish to exclude.
3.  **Step 3: Complete:** A final confirmation will appear. Click "Confirm Import" to add the transactions permanently to your account.

<a id="data-management-statements"></a>
### Generate Statements (`/accounts/statement`)
Create a formal, professional-looking statement for any of your accounts.
1.  Select the **Account**.
2.  Choose the **Export Format** (PDF or XLSX).
3.  Filter by a **Date Range** or by the **Latest 'N' Transactions**.
4.  Click **"Generate Statement"**.

<a id="data-management-export"></a>
### Exporting Your Data
1.  Go to the main **Transactions** page.
2.  Use the **Filters** to create the exact dataset you need.
3.  Click the **Export** button and choose your format (XLSX or CSV).
